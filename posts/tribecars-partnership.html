Travelhorse and Tribecars Launch Rent and Earn Program

30 DEC 2020 - Travelhorse and Tribecars today announced their partnership to launch the Rent and Earn program. Under this scheme, freelancers, work-from-home professionals and those seeking temporary gigs are able to participate in the growing need of delivery service personnel.

The Rent and Earn taps on the car rental economy to grow the delivery driver base and has a ready fleet of vehicles that are immediately available. Those aged 23 to 65 with valid drivers licence may apply for accreditation.

Registration is simply three steps on this website - https://www.tribecar.com/rent-and-earn/travelhorse
