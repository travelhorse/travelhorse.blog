Annoucing the Travelhorse and Tribe Tours Partnership

Today, we are excited to announce Tribe Tours as the latest Travelhorse logistics dash partner in Singapore!

About Tribe Tours
Tribe is a local boutique tour operator that provides privately guided tours of Singapore. They believe in having an active tourist experience and creating tours that involve engagement and interaction. From snapping pictures of local architecture to cooking local dishes, Tribe offers local tour ideas for corporate events, educational and professional institutions and more.

A Uniquely Singapore Experience - with a rich local heritage
Tribe Tours joins our global travel partners in working with Travelhorse to make their customers enjoy a more curated and personalized experience of Singapore.

As part of the SingapoRediscover initiative, Tribe will continue to support local tourism by not only helping locals to discover the hidden gems of Singapore, but also supporting local businesses by telling stories of their passions and products. Therefore, it is launching a fortnightly live stream online tour that brings viewers behind the scenes of family run businesses and heritage brands on Friday, 7 August 2020! Join Tribe Tours and a family run curry puff institution for a Curry Puff Factory Livestream. Learn about the different types of curry puffs, watch how they are made and #supportlocalbusinesses by purchasing the curry puff bundles.

Our Partnership
The Travelhorse and Tribe Tours partnership is a no brainer! Now, Tribe Tours can continue their signature tour experiences despite the current situation and their customers can enjoy the convenience of simply tuning into the livestream while enjoyinh the local snacks that are delivered to their doorsteps by Travelhorse.

Travelhorse's Dash Services
Access to convenient, rich and delicious local snacks while exploring Singapore from the comfort of home is made possible thanks to our easy-to-use Dash services. Travelhorse has emerged out of the coronavirus by rallying local initiatives and offer f&b dash services to its jockeys and merchants. The endorsement by the Enterprise Singapore as part of the food booster package also provide us with more leverage. We look forward to building more partnerships in the future.
