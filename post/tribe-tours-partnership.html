Travelhorse and Tribe Tours Ink Partnership To Boost Local Tourism and Support Local Businesses

Travelhorse and Tribe Tours today announced their collaboration in support of local tourism and businesses. As part of the SingapoRediscover initiative, Tribe will continue to promote tourism by helping locals discover the hidden gems of Singapore. Travelhorse complements this with its dash services delivering products of local businesses to the doorsteps of residents islandwide. Together, Travelhorse and Tribe will support local businesses by telling stories of their passions and products while making them easily available to consumers.

The partnershipâ€™s initial collaboration is a fortnightly live stream beginning on 7 August 2020 at 10.30am through Tribe Tours' website. This first feature will bring viewers a unique Singapore experience behind the scenes of family run curry puff business. Viewers will learn about the history of the business, different aspects of production as well as learn more about the different types of the popular local delicacy. Additionally, viewers would be able to #supportlocalbusinesses by ordering curry puff bundles and have them delivered by Travelhorse.

Tribe Tours will continue its signature curated experiences despite the pandemic and their customers can enjoy the convenience of simply tuning into the livestream while savouring the local snacks that are delivered to their doorsteps by Travelhorse.

About Tribe Tours
Tribe is a local boutique tour operator that provides privately guided tours of Singapore. They believe in having an active tourist experience and creating tours that involve engagement and interaction. From snapping pictures of local architecture to cooking local dishes, Tribe offers local tour ideas for corporate events, educational and professional institutions and more.
